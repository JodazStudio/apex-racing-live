

## Fast Sport Timing — Chrono-Precision Landing Page

A premium sports timing landing page in Spanish with the "Chrono-Precision" design system: matte white surfaces, bold International Orange accents, hard-edged geometric components, and dynamic typography.

### Design System
- **Light technical theme**: Cool white background, deep charcoal text, International Orange (`hsl(12, 90%, 50%)`) as primary accent
- **Hard edges** (`rounded-none`), `1px` technical borders, sharp mechanical shadows
- **Fonts**: Satoshi for headings (bold, stretched, uppercase), Geist Sans for body, Geist Mono for data/numbers
- **`tabular-nums`** on all timing data, tight letter-spacing on headings

### Sections (Top to Bottom)

1. **Top Navigation Bar** — Ticker-tape style nav with placeholder logo, links: Inicio, Servicios, Nosotros, Eventos, Resultados, Contacto

2. **Hero Section** — Full-width with dark overlay background (CSS gradient, no image). Large dynamic headline: "PRECISIÓN EN LA LÍNEA DE META". Subheadline about timing services. Two CTA buttons (mechanical style with orange shadow). Orange "loading needle" animation at top.

3. **Stats Bar** — 4-column row with key metrics in Geist Mono: "100% Cobertura", "RFID Tecnología", "Live Resultados", "24/7 Soporte". Hard borders between items.

4. **About Section** — "Quiénes Somos" badge, heading about technology and passion for sports. Bullet points with orange markers. Blueprint-grid background lines.

5. **Services Grid** — 6 service cards in 3x2 grid. Each card: white with 4px orange left-border, technical icon (Lucide), title + description. Services: Digital Timing, Registration Platform, Online Results, Manual Timing, Logistics, Professional Certification.

6. **Upcoming Events** — 2-3 event cards with date, name, location, and "Inscribirse" CTA button. Data displayed in mono font.

7. **Contact CTA** — "¿Organizas un evento deportivo?" with phone number, email, WhatsApp button (mechanical style), and email button.

8. **Footer** — 4-column layout: Brand info, Services links, Contact details, Legal links. Bottom copyright bar.

### Interactions
- Mechanical button hover: `translate-x-[2px] translate-y-[2px]` with reduced shadow
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Stats counter animation on scroll into view
